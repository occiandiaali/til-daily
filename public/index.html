🔥 Fact of the Day

Cleopatra lived closer in time to the Moon landing than to the construction of the Great Pyramid

The Great Pyramid of Giza was completed around 2560 BCE. Cleopatra VII was born in 69 BCE — roughly 2,500 years after the pyramid was built. Yet the Apollo 11 Moon landing happened in 1969 CE, only about 2,000 years after Cleopatra's time. The pyramids are so ancient that Cleopatra herself was closer to us than to the builders of her civilization's most iconic monuments.
